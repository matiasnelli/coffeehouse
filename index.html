Our Heritage

Our story begins in 1971 along the cobblestone streets of Seattle’s historic Pike Place Market. It was here where Coffee House opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Ten years later, a young New Yorker named Howard Schultz would walk through these doors and become captivated with Coffee House from his first sip. After joining the company in 1982, a different cobblestone road would lead him to another discovery. It was on a trip to Milan in 1983 that Howard first experienced Italy’s coffeehouses, and he returned to Seattle inspired to bring the warmth and artistry of its coffee culture.
